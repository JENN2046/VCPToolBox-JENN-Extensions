# ==========================================
# PROMPT KNOWLEDGE BASE
# ==========================================
## 质量增强词
- 基础：masterpiece, best quality
- 增强：ultra-detailed, 8k, highres, sharp focus
- 写实：photorealistic, professional
- 艺术：cinematic lighting, dramatic

## 负面提示词
- 通用：(worst quality, low quality, normal quality:1.7), lowres, blurry
- 电商：watermark, text, signature, logo
- 人像：ugly, deformed, bad anatomy, extra limbs

## 场景关键词
- 纯色：studio, white background, clean
- 街头：urban street, city, modern
- 咖啡厅：cafe, cozy, warm lighting
- 办公室：office, corporate, professional
- 海滩：beach, sunset, tropical
